# Dreaming Spanish Enhancer — Feature Roadmap

## Almost Done Tab Enhancements

- [ ] **Sort by difficulty score** — let users sort section completers and nearly finished videos by difficulty, not just by progress/completion
- [ ] **Time estimate to complete** — show "~45 min to finish this section" based on remaining unwatched video durations
- [ ] **Streak tracker** — highlight sections where the user has been consistently watching (e.g., "watched 3 this week")

## Browse / Discovery Features

- [ ] **Difficulty range filter** — overlay a difficulty slider on the browse page so you can filter to your comfort zone (e.g., 40-60 only)
- [ ] **"Next up" recommendation** — based on watch history patterns (preferred guides, topics, difficulty sweet spot), suggest the next video to watch
- [ ] **Unwatched count badges** — show how many unwatched videos remain per series/topic directly on the browse page cards

## Stats & Insights

- [ ] **Difficulty progression chart** — graph your average video difficulty over time to visualize how you're leveling up
- [ ] **Weekly/monthly digest** — summary of hours watched, sections completed, average difficulty tackled vs. previous period
- [ ] **Level readiness indicator** — "You've watched 80% of Intermediate content at difficulty 50+, you might be ready for Advanced"
- [ ] **Guide diversity stats** — show which guides you watch most/least to encourage variety

## Quality of Life

- [ ] **Keyboard shortcuts** on the progress page (j/k to navigate sections, enter to open video)
- [ ] **Export progress** — CSV/JSON dump of watch history and stats
- [ ] **Dark mode sync** — match the extension's UI to the DS site's theme if they ever add one
- [ ] **Bookmark/pin videos** — mark specific unwatched videos as "watch next" across any tab
